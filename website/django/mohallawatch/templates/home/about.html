What is Mohalla Watch?

Mohalla Watch is an online service that informs it's users about day-to-day disruptions in areas and neighbourhoods that the user traverses everyday and/or cares about in the city of Karachi. For now, it parses live twitter data to find the latest happenings and sends a SMS to a user if there is a potentially disruptive activity in a user's area of interest.

All collected data is available for parsing on our website.

For now this ia a prototype that is being tested in Karachi.
